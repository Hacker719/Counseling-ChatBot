Terapeut

The online therapist

Terapeut is the online therapist open to anyone. Terapeut enables you to speak your feelings, without having to have someone on the other side. This enables more people to access therapy at a time without waiting for live asssistance. Terapeut also allows people to do this anonymously, or create an accout to keep track of their mental health over a long time period.
